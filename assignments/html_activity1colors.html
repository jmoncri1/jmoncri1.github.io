Learning About Colors

The purpose of this webpage is to briefly outline the primary and secondary colors.

Primary Colors

Primary colors are colors that can be combined to form more colors. The primary colors are:

  • Red
  • Blue
  • Yellow

Secondary Colors

Secondary colors are the colors that can be made combining primary colors.

  • Green
  • Orange
  • Purple

In summary, the primary colors red, yellow, and blue can be combined various ways to form secondary colors green, orange, and purple.
